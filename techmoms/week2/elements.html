How To Make Italian Pasta Salad

    Ingredients

  • 1 box of vegetable rotini
  • 1 bottle of Italian salad dressing
  • 1 package of shredded cheese of your choice
  • 1 can of black olives
  • 1 small package of bacon bits
  • 1 small package of pepperoni
  • small bag of frozen peas

    Steps

  1. Start cooking your pasta according to the directions on the box/bag
  2. While your pasta is cooking start cutting the pepperoni and the olives into halves or quarters
  3. Put the cut up ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Add about a third of the bag of frozen peas to the bowl, uncooked
  5. Add half of the bag of bacon bits to the bowl
  6. Once the pasta is done, rinse it in cold water until the pasta is cooled off, drain it, then add it to the bowl
  7. Add 1/3-1/2 bag of the shredded cheese and 3/4 of the bottle of italian dressing to the bowl and mix
  8. Now you are done **Pasta Salad better served cold**
